Love Often!

November 29 2022

I had chills down my spine
You see, I was just about to kiss her
After I had had a sipe of my wine
But the morning breeze woke me up
Somebody left open the window blind
I feel I've fallen in love
As I feast now on the brilliance of ripe sunshine
Embracing me with its comforting warmth
Takes me back to the good ol' time
Past the heartbreaks. Past the ugly banter
It's a message from the divine
To love, to hug and to kiss more often
I've fallen so far behind

— Yours Truly! (picture credit to @karan)
